# Proactive Outreach To Recently Funded Companies

Use this workflow when no qualifying live job is found or when a company is likely to hire soon but has not posted the target role yet.

## Why This Works

Recently funded companies often enter a 30-90 day hiring cycle before every senior role is public. A concise, evidence-based message can surface an unposted role or create a warm conversation.

## Source Requirements

Use public, verifiable sources only:

- Funding announcements from credible news sources.
- VC portfolio pages.
- Company blog/newsroom posts.
- Founder or executive posts.
- Startup databases.
- Career pages showing adjacent hiring activity.

Do not claim a funding round, headcount, investor, product launch, or executive name unless it is verified by a live source.

## Outreach Message Template — LinkedIn

```text
Hi [Name],

Congrats on [verified company milestone]. I saw [specific source-backed detail] and thought the timing might be relevant.

I am [candidate name], a [target positioning] with experience in [verified experience area 1] and [verified experience area 2]. [One sentence connecting verified candidate experience to the company's public direction.]

If you are planning [target role/function] as the team scales, I would be glad to compare notes for 20 minutes.

Best,
[Name]
```

## Execution Checklist

```text
[ ] Verify funding/company signal with a live source.
[ ] Confirm target geography/work mode is plausible.
[ ] Check career page for matching posted roles.
[ ] Check tracker for duplicate outreach.
[ ] Identify founder, CTO, VP, recruiter, or hiring manager from public sources.
[ ] Draft one concise message using only verified facts.
[ ] Add tracker row with status proactive_outreach_pending.
[ ] Follow up once after the configured wait period.
```
