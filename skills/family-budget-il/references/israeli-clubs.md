# Israeli benefit & discount clubs → recommendations

Use this to turn a family's club memberships (and gaps) into concrete monthly
savings. **Perks change constantly — web-search the specific clubs the family
named for current benefits before quoting any number.** This file is a map of
*what exists and where it helps*, not a list of current discounts.

## How to recommend
1. Look at where the family actually spends (top categories from the dashboard).
2. Match each big category to the clubs/tools that cut it.
3. For each match, web-search the current benefit, then state the expected
   monthly ₪ saving in plain terms.
4. Flag clubs they're *eligible for but not using*. Keep it to 3–5 actions.
5. Stay non-pushy and note you're not a financial advisor (esp. for credit
   products and anything with fees or interest).

## Major programs (search before quoting specifics)

**Workplace / sector clubs**
- **חבר (Hever)** — career military, security forces, and some public-sector
  staff. Strong on vacations, flights, electronics, gift cards. Big win for the
  *Travel* and *Shopping* categories.
- **ועד עובדים / הסתדרות** — works-council and Histadrut member discounts:
  vacations, attractions, retail, sometimes supermarket vouchers.
- **בהצדעה** and reserve-duty benefit programs — periodic perks for reservists.

**Meal / benefit cards (employer-provided)**
- **תן ביס / 10bis**, **Cibus**, **Pluxee (ex-Sodexo)**, **Goodi** — pre-tax-ish
  meal allowances. Maps to *Food → restaurants/delivery*. If an employer offers
  one and the family isn't enrolled, that's free money.
- Gift-card platforms (**BuyMe**, **Pluxee gift**) — useful for *Gifts*.

**Credit-card clubs & cashback**
- Card issuers **Max**, **Cal**, **Isracard** and bank clubs (**לאומי**,
  **פועלים**, **דיסקונט**, **מזרחי**) run benefit clubs, cashback, and
  category multipliers. Match the right card to the biggest category (groceries,
  fuel, online). Watch annual fees vs. benefit.
- Fuel cards / discounts (Paz Yellow, Sonol, Delek clubs) → *Transport → fuel*.

**Supermarket / retail loyalty**
- **מועדון שופרסל**, **ויקטורי**, **יוחננוף**, **יינות ביתן**, **אושר עד**,
  **חצי חינם** — loyalty pricing and coupons → *Food → supermarket*.
- Home/DIY: **הום סנטר**, **ACE** clubs → *Shopping → furniture & home*.

**Utilities & subscriptions**
- Periodically re-shop electricity (private suppliers), internet+TV bundles
  (בזק/HOT/partner/cellcom), and mobile plans. Subscriptions audit: cancel
  duplicate streaming, annual-vs-monthly switches → *Subscriptions* /
  *Entertainment* / *Housing → internet/phone*.

**Savings / pension side (hand off, don't advise)**
- For provident-fund / pension / קרן השתלמות fee and yield questions, use the
  **gemelnet-advisor** skill if available. Management-fee negotiation is often
  the single biggest, lowest-effort win — but it's an advisory topic; present
  data, don't direct.

## Output shape for a recommendation
A short, scannable block, e.g.:

> **Where you can trim (this month):**
> - **Groceries (₪3,100/mo):** your Shufersal club + [card] cashback → ~₪___/mo.
> - **Eating out (₪1,400/mo):** you have Cibus but aren't maxing it → ~₪___/mo.
> - **Not using:** you're eligible for חבר — biggest lever on your July trip.

Fill the blanks from live search, not memory.
